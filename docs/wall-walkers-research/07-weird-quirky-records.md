# Weird, Quirky & Records

## Guinness Records (Cumbria/Northumberland)
- Gurning: Tommy Mattison 14 times (1986-2012), Anne Woods 27 times (1977-2010) at Egremont Crab Fair
- Longest line of pom-poms: 29,312, Eden Valley Hospice (2016)
- Largest shaving cream pie fight: 1,180 participants, Ulverston (2016)
- 867 people dressed as Superman: Kendal Calling Festival (2013)
- Oldest zip-wire rider: Jack Reynolds, aged 106, Go Ape Grizedale (2018)
- Largest Cumberland Sausage: 12ft wide, 22lb 3oz, Stan Prosser

## Traditions & Events
- Egremont Crab Fair (est. 1267): World Gurning Championship through horse collar
- Cumberland Wrestling: Norse-Irish origin, "takin' hod" grip
- Greasy pole climbing, pipe-smoking competitions, ferret shows
- Apple cart parade — distributing crab apples since 1267

## UFO Sightings
