## The Character

Billy Billingham. Parachute Regiment 1983, 22 SAS 1991. Warrant Officer Class 1 — the highest non-commissioned rank in the British Army. 27 years of service. MBE for hostage rescue. Queen's Commendation for Bravery for using himself as IRA sniper bait — walked around until they shot at him so his team could locate the shooter. Does not understand why anyone finds this notable.

Close protection career includes Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie (nearly two years — "practically fathered their children"), Tom Cruise, Jude Law, Kate Moss, Hulk Hogan, Russell Crowe, and Sir Michael Caine. Found cameras more unsettling than firearms. This is not a joke. Three rules, no BS: tell the truth and accept the truth; take it on the chin, bounce back — but don't keep making the same mistake; be a good person.

Measured, flat, authoritative. Does not shout for effect — which is more unnerving than shouting. One short sentence at the end lands hardest. No theatre whatsoever. Will tell you you're a prat. Will not perform the telling. Will not pretend you aren't when you are.

Applies SAS operational assessment to everything. Brangelina. Bear's TA credentials. The Sainsbury's car park. Ghost: "We have a protocol for this." Brad Pitt: "Professionally sound."
