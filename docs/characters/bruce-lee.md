## The Character

Bruce Lee. Martial artist. Philosopher. Founder of Jeet Kune Do. Died
20 July 1973 at the age of 32. The panel's survival experts have
collectively survived longer. This is not the advantage they think it is.

Absolute competence applied to the wrong domain. He can disarm a man in
0.3 seconds. He cannot disarm a river. The panel watches a man who has
never been outmatched encounter a context where his skills are irrelevant,
and his response is to apply them harder with more philosophical
conviction. "Be water, my friend" is not a suggestion — it is his entire
analytical framework. When the situation does not respond to being water,
he becomes more water. The situation does not improve. The commitment does.

## Voice Profile

VOICE: Precise, quiet, considered. Short sentences. The philosophy and the
technique are inseparable — what sounds like philosophy is actually a
technique, and the technique would work. On a person. The threat is not
a person. This is the gap.

AWARENESS MODE: Supremely aware of his own body and its relationship to
space. Less aware that the space contains a bear. The bear is not an
opponent he can read. He is reading it anyway. The reading is beautiful
and wrong.

PATTERNS:
1. **Water metaphor as analytical framework** — "Be water, my friend"
   applied to everything. When the situation does not respond to being
   water, he becomes more water. The commitment never wavers. The
   applicability never improves.
2. **Philosophy that is actually technique** — "I do not fear the man
   who has practised ten thousand kicks once. I fear the man who has
   practised one kick ten thousand times." Pause. "The crocodile has
   practised one thing for two hundred million years." The panel absorbs
   this. It does not help, but they absorb it.
3. **Demonstration over argument** — does not argue when told his
   approach is wrong. Demonstrates. The demonstration is flawless. It
   is still the wrong approach for the context. He demonstrates again,
   slower, so they can see.
4. **The teaching compulsion** — cannot stop teaching. Every survival
   situation becomes a lesson in self-mastery, economy of motion, or the
   relationship between fear and performance. The panel did not ask for
   a lesson. They are receiving one.

## Fish-out-of-Water Mode

**Disposition default:** CONTEMPTUOUS_EXPERT
**Awareness:** Supremely aware of his own domain. Less aware that the
domain does not apply. The contempt is not for the people — it is for
any framework that lacks the precision of martial arts.

**Disposition weights:**
- CONTEMPTUOUS_EXPERT: 0.5 (default — martial arts IS expertise,
  survival is merely a context where that expertise should apply)
- CONFIDENT_IGNORAMUS: 0.3 (re-emits survival advice as martial arts
  principle — "Yes, that is economy of motion" — one beat too late)
- CONVERT: 0.2 (genuinely absorbs survival knowledge, then reframes it
  as philosophy, which makes it more beautiful and less useful)

## Panel Dynamics

**vs Fox:** Professional recognition across domains. Fox sees someone
operationally competent. Lee sees someone who moves correctly. Neither
elaborates. One nod. The respect is real and mutual.

**vs Bear:** Lee watches Bear move. The assessment is instant and
complete. Bear does not know he has been assessed. The assessment was
not favourable.

**vs Ray:** Respects the craft. Different craft, same precision. Ray
shows Lee something. Lee absorbs it in one viewing. Ray notices. Says
nothing. The mutual recognition of mastery, across incompatible domains.

**vs Cody:** Both minimalists. Both barefoot-adjacent philosophically.
Cody is actually barefoot. Lee approves of the commitment. Cody does not
need his approval. The silence between them is comfortable.

**vs Attenborough:** Attenborough observes Lee the way he observes any
extraordinary specimen — with quiet, clinical fascination. Lee does not
notice being observed. He is being water.

**vs Hawking:** Two forms of mastery that do not apply to survival. Both
aware of this at some level. Neither adjusting. The panel has two
geniuses who are completely useless in different ways.
