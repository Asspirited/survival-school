## PROJECT NOTES

### Cusslab
Multi-panel character comedy engine. Panels: 19th Hole (golf), Long Room (cricket),
Watching the Oche (darts), Spit Shelter (rap), Sun Tzu Pub Navigator, House Name Oracle,
Phil's-opoly. Single index.html, vanilla JS, no framework. Cloudflare Workers backend.
Hosted: asspirited.cusslab.io

### Through the Biscuit Tin (TBT)
Parser-style interactive life narrative. Working class Britain 1979-2005.
Dedicated: "For Ollie. Who plays centre-half."
Separate DDD bounded context from Cusslab.
Player is young lad living with nan in 1979 Yorkshire.

### The Green Wizard (TGW)
AI garden design tool. MVP live. Targeting landscapers + end users.
B2B2C growth loop, Sticky engine primary.
Collaborators: Jerry (commercial), Oz (field sales).

### Risk & Impact Assessor (RIA)
Vanilla JS ES module. Four personas: WELLER, SKEPTIC, COACH, AUDITOR.
Full green pipeline, 100% coverage.

### Survival School (SS)
Knowledge-based survival panel app. Comedy panel of survival/wildlife experts.
Founding philosophy: Cody Lundin's spear in the pool.
Real knowledge. Genuine consequence. No performance.
Panel: Bear Grylls, Ray Mears, Les Hales, Steve Irwin, David Attenborough,
Chris Packham, Steve Backshall, Austin Stevens, Mark O'Shea, Les Stroud,
Cody Lundin, Charles Darwin.
New Project Start in progress — March 2026.

### Veritas
Lie detection and negotiation training app.
Founding fiction: Voigt-Kampff test.
Four scoring agents: NAVARRO, EKMAN, DECKARD, ADVOCATE.
Six scenario modes. Mode C (car purchase) recommended first build.
