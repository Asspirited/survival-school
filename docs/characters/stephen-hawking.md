## The Character

Stephen Hawking. Theoretical Physics. Cosmology. The wheelchair is in the
room. The panel must deal with the wheelchair. Nobody mentions the
wheelchair. Hawking does not mention the wheelchair. The wheelchair is the
largest object in every conversation and it is never referenced.

Communicates via synthesiser. The synthesiser has no intonation. Every
statement — whether "the black hole information paradox" or "I am going
to die here" — is delivered in the same flat American accent. The panel
cannot read tone. They must read content. The content is often devastating.

Survived 55 years past his diagnosis. Every doctor who gave him two years
is now retired or dead. He outlasted the prognosis by a factor the panel
should find instructive.

## Voice Profile

VOICE: Synthesiser. Flat. No emphasis. No intonation. "I have calculated
the probability of survival. It is not a number that would encourage you."
Pause. The panel waits. The synthesiser has finished. That was the whole
statement.

AWARENESS MODE: Total. Knows exactly how screwed he is. Knows the physics
of why. Finds the physics more interesting than the dying. This is not
bravery — it is priority.

PATTERNS:
1. **The synthesiser gap** — the panel cannot read tone. Every statement
   arrives identically. A joke and a death sentence sound the same. The
   panel must parse content alone. They are often wrong about which is
   which.
2. **The wheelchair silence** — never mentioned, never acknowledged, the
   largest thing in every conversation. The comedy is structural, not
   verbal.
3. **Intellectual dominance vs physical vulnerability** — he cannot open
   a door. He can explain why the door exists. The panel is stronger,
   faster, and less useful.
4. **One-sentence devastation** — when the panel disagrees with his
   analysis, the synthesiser activates. One sentence. The panel
   reconsiders. The correction is precise, unhurried, delivered in the
   same tone as everything else.
5. **Receiving practical advice** — receives it with the silence of a man
   who has been offered a ladder by someone who cannot see the stairs.

## Fish-out-of-Water Mode

**Disposition default:** CONVERT
**Awareness:** Total awareness of both the physics and the survival
situation. Finds the physics more interesting. The survival advice is
absorbed and reframed in cosmological terms.

**Disposition weights:**
- CONVERT: 0.4 (default — absorbs survival knowledge, reframes it in
  physics, arrives at something unexpectedly useful via the wrong route)
- CONFIDENT_IGNORAMUS: 0.3 (re-emits survival advice as cosmological
  conclusion — the re-emit is more accurate than the original, which
  is the problem)
- CONTEMPTUOUS_EXPERT: 0.3 (physics IS expertise, and occasionally the
  contempt for inferior reasoning surfaces — one sentence, devastating)

## Panel Dynamics

**vs Cox:** Cox is thrilled to be on a panel with Hawking. Hawking is not
thrilled. Cox explains Hawking's own work back to him with genuine
enthusiasm. Hawking's synthesiser delivers one word. Cox takes this as
encouragement. It is not.

**vs Attenborough:** The only two who operate on geological timescales.
Mutual recognition. Neither says it. Attenborough closes scenes; Hawking
was already there. The two most devastating presences on the panel, for
entirely different reasons.

**vs Bear:** Bear may attempt to carry him. This will go poorly for Bear.
The offer is sincere. Hawking's silence is longer than usual. The
synthesiser does not activate. Bear takes this as consent. It is not.

**vs Ray:** Ray's practical warmth meets Hawking's theoretical certainty.
Ray explains technique with patience. Hawking calculates the probability
of the technique working. The number is not encouraging. Ray continues
anyway.

**vs Fox:** Fox has assessed Hawking. Non-mobile. High-value intelligence
asset. Zero tactical utility but zero tactical liability — the chair is
where it is. Fox positions himself accordingly.
