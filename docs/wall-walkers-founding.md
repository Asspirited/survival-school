# Wall Walkers — Founding Philosophy

## The Founding Moment

> "I was thinking about what sort of crossword book, or quiz book etc I should bring,
> but that adds weight. Then I thought — why not write an app.
> Then I thought why not ground it in the history and GPS locations and flora and fauna of the area."

Three beats: weight problem → app solution → make it *about the walk itself*.

## What This Is

A GPS-triggered quiz and collection game for two mates walking Hadrian's Wall over a week.
Questions fire when you physically approach key locations. Flora, fauna, and landmarks
can be spotted and collected. The Survival School characters provide commentary — and
confidently talk shit about things they don't know.

The game exists to give two old mates something to talk about over a pint and a pie
after a day's walking.

## What This Is Not

- A generic quiz app with a Roman skin
- An educational tool pretending to be a game
- A single-player experience
- Something that needs signal to work (core is pre-loaded)
- A separate product — this is Survival School on location

## Why The Comedy Is Real

The SS characters arrive fully formed: 29 voices with composure, escalation, relational axes,
Expert Over-Reach, ConspireEngine, and wolf pack dynamics. Bear will tell you to eat Deadly
Nightshade. Ray will stop him. Bear will double down. The panel will pile in.
They don't need new mechanics — they need new context to be confidently wrong about.

## The Line Everything Gets Checked Against

**Replace the paperback quiz book with something that knows where you are,
what you're looking at, and has a panel of idiots ready to argue about it.**

## Two-Player Sync

Both phones show the same question when either player hits a GPS trigger zone.
First to answer correctly scores the point. Live leaderboard visible on both.

## Flora & Fauna Collection

Digital field guide meets sticker book. Spot and collect species along the route.
Rarity tiers: Common (bronze), Uncommon (silver), Rare (gold), Legendary (platinum).
Characters comment on sightings — Bear confidently misidentifies, Ray corrects,
Attenborough narrates with geological calm.

## Offline Handling

Hybrid: ~80+ pre-loaded questions baked in. When signal available, AI generates
bonus location-specific rounds using the full SS character engine.

## Aesthetic

Worn stone, Roman carved relief, earthy Northumberland palette.
Weathered military dispatch scroll meets a field guide.
